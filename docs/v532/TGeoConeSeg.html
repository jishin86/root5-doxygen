class TGeoConeSeg: public TGeoCone

 TGeoCone - conical tube  class. It has 5 parameters :
            dz - half length in z
            Rmin1, Rmax1 - inside and outside radii at -dz
            Rmin2, Rmax2 - inside and outside radii at +dz
